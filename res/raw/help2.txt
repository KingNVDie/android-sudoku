                       Rules:
The objective is to fill a 9x9 grid so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3x3 boxes (also called blocks or regions) contains the digits from 1 to 9 only one time each. The puzzle setter provides a partially completed grid.
                       How to play:
The main square is highlighted by red color, with the help joystick it is possible to move it. To put digit in the highlighted section, press the necessary figure on the keyboard.
If you use joystick, press it. The bottom list will be illuminated and in it with the help of joystick it is possible to choose the necessary figure. 
You can use the hint by pressing the spacebar.
