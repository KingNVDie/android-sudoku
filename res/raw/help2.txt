                       Rules:
The objective is to fill a 9x9 grid so that each column, each row,
and each of the nine 3x3 boxes (also called blocks or regions) contains
the digits from 1 to 9 only one time each. The puzzle setter provides a
partially completed grid.
                       How to play:
The main square is highlighted by red color, with the help joystick
it is possible to move it. To put figure in the highlighted section,
press the necessary figure on the keyboard or press on joystick,
The bottom list will be illuminated and in it with the help
joystick it is possible to choose the necessary figure. Once again 
We click on joystick and the figure becomes in the necessary place.
The blank - in the main cell appears correct value.
